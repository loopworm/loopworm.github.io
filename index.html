Limited quality ingredients

Over 5.5 Billion people are dependent on Meat, fish & eggs for Protein, the demand of which would grow by 70% in 30 years. High quality ingredients are required for better growth & nutrition in Livestock. Such sources are limited & thus alternative sustainable sources are needed to ensure food security.

Unsustainable conventional ingredients

90% Seas & Oceans are overfished due to uncontrolled Fishmeal production. These WIld Forage fish are a part of the ecosystem that provides 70% of the Oxygen we breathe. Other conventional ingredients lead to Environmental stress on our forests, fresh water bodies and arable land.

Wasted agri ingredients

Global annual food waste is around 1.3 Billion tonnes. Organic By-products from Food Processing industries, Animal rejects & Crop residues are still getting wasted & are largely unutilised. The same can be processed to valuable products both for food and non food purposes through innovations in Biotechnology & Lifesciences.

High import dependency

Global demand for Proteins & Fats have led to larger incidences for shortages in supplies of commoditised feed ingredients, leading to higher cost of production & inflation in protein dense foodstuff. Dependence on specific countries for imports is dangerous due to Geopolitical scenarios & natural calamities.

About us

Loopworm is an Agri-Biotechnology company that takes inspiration from nature, to convert organic wastes into valuable products promoting health, nourishment & wellbeing for all forms of life. Through novel Bioscience, Chemistry & Technology, we are utilising Insects to produce novel Bio based products. Insects are natural detritivores and a key part of the Ecosystem often ignored for their usefulness and considered pest & nuisance. They are a rich source of proteins, fats, and micronutrients, and an essential part of the natural diets of both land & aquatic fauna. They are an excellent source of high-quality Amino Acids, Fatty Acids, Peptides & Bio-molecules like Melanin & Chitin, having varied applications in Cosmetics, Nutraceuticals & Biopharmaceutical industries. Loopworm is currently focused on producing alternative sustainable protein & fats for Shrimp Feeds, Poultry Feeds & Pet Foods. Highly digestible Amino Acids & insect-like natural smell in the protein concentrate lead to superior palatability in insect-based Aquaculture Feeds. Insect oil is a highly sustainable alternative to conventional feed oil sources like Palm Oil, Fish Oil,& Krill Oil. Inclusion of insect products in Poultry feeds leads to better Egg & Meat quality, higher productivity, better immunity, and enhanced welfare for commercially farmed birds. Hypoallergenic properties & natural bio-availability of anti-microbial supplements, cartilage & bone growth promoters lead to superior health for Dogs & Cats. The United Nations & the FAO consider ‘Insects - The Future of Food & Feed’, but with the visionary use of modern science & engineering, Insects can truly help us bring a new dimension to novel biotechnology & bio-pharmaceutical applications.

Why Insects?

Rich in nutrients

Insects contain all essential & Non essential amino acids, are a rich source of Important PUFAs & Bio-active peptides ensuring productivity, growth & immunity benefits for farmed animals as well as Anti-aging & Anti-allergic benefits for Pets.

Sustainable in nature

Insect protein production requires 170X lesser land and 200X lesser water in comparison to Soy protein production. They are a scalable sustainable solution to reduce the stress on Wild Marine Fish population, arable land and potable water.

Limitless in applications

Insects are rich in Anti-microbial Peptides, Melanin, Chitin & other high value Bio-molecules that can help develop novel solutions for Health, Cosmetics, Nutraceuticals, & Bio-Pharma Industries.

Circularity in food-chain

Insects are the natural food for Fish & Birds. They are natural detritivores & consume organic by-products to grow, further becoming a dense source of Proteins, Fats, & other Nutrients for organisms in higher trophic levels.

Products

Loop-Meal

LoopMeal is a partially defatted insect protein concentrate or insect meal with ~60% Crude protein (DM). They come in two grades, with ~15% fats and with < 10% fats. LoopMeal has a protein digetibility score of >85%. It is rich in Bio-active peptides promoting Anti-oxidative, Anti-inflammatory, and Anti-microbial properties. Thus, it can be used as a functional ingredient in Shrimp, Fish, Poultry & Pet food formulations, replacing expensive ingredients like Fish Meal or Krill meal effectively. The insect like smell enhances palatability and a lower ash content leads to higher digestibility in Livestock & Pets.

Loop-Oil

LoopOil is derived by purifying the crude oil extracted in the solvent free process of LoopMeal production. Primarily consisting of C:16, C:18, C:18 n:1, C:20 and C:18 n:3 (Omega 3 Fatty Acids), it is the perfect fat source for animals. It’s best served as an energy source, either mixed right into pet food or animal feed or used as a tasty coating (like a post-extrusion spray) applied for enhanced palatability.

Loop-Grubs

LoopGrubs are a highly sustainable & nutritious feed. The grubs are fed a 100% plant-based diet and are Anti-biotics free. They have high-quality protein, a higher fat content and unique fatty acid profile. With less than 5% Ash content, the whole dried insects are highly digestible and have natural chemo-attractant properties to attract fish/ birds ensuring maximum growth & the highest standard of animal welfare.

Loop-Frass

LoopFrass is a natural soil amendment and is a blend of food fibers, insect skins & droppings. It is rich in minerals, chitin and organic matter that helps restore the soil quality. The chitin helps build immunity against pets and fungus. It has high water holding capacity and promotes the growth of beneficial micro-organisms, assisting in plant growth and better root:shoot ratio.
