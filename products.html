    Loop-Meal


    Loop-Meal is a partially defatted insect protein concentrate or insect meal with ~60% Crude protein (DM). It is rich in Bio-active peptides promoting Anti-oxidative, Anti-inflammatory, and Anti-microbial properties. Thus, it can be used as a functional ingredient in Aqua, Poultry & Pet food formulations, replacing Fish Meal or Krill meal effectively. The insect-like smell enhances palatability and a lower ash content leads to higher digestibility in Livestock & Pets.

    • All Essential Amino Acids




    • Easily digestible proteins (>85%)




    • Superior palatability with very low Ash content




    • Hypoallergenic properties in pet food

    Loop-Grubs


    LoopGrubs are a highly sustainable & nutritious feed. The grubs are fed a 100% plant-based diet and are Anti-biotics free. They have high-quality protein, a higher fat content and unique fatty acid profile. With less than 5% Ash content, the whole dried insects are highly digestible and have natural chemo-attractant properties to attract fish/ birds ensuring maximum growth & the highest standard of animal welfare.

    • High-quality amino acids, lipids and micro nutrients




    • High digestibility with <5% Ash Content




    • Anti-Biotics Free & 100% Plant Fed




    • Slow Dried for better taste & quality

    Loop-Frass


    • Balances soil pH




    • Enhances plant immunity




    • Increases soil water holding capacity




    • Slow nutrient bioavailability

    LoopFrass is a natural soil amendment and is a blend of food fibers, insect skins & droppings. It is rich in minerals, chitin and organic matter that helps restore the soil quality. The chitin helps build immunity against pets and fungus. It has high water holding capacity and promotes the growth of beneficial micro-organisms, assisting in plant growth and better root:shoot ratio.
